Nodhumi Tamizh

Research Center

Nodhumi Tamil Research Center is a specialized and dedicated platform for the study of language and linguistics. It is a hub for linguistic research, language education, and language-related services. This Center employs linguists, language experts and language researchers who conduct researches, analyze data, and develop new theories and methodologies related to the language.
