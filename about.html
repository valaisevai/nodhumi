Our Story

Nodhumi Tamizh Research Center was initiated to develop the research field in Tamizh language. This Research Center mainly concentrates on Tamizh literature, Tamizh computing, technological development on Tamizh and also creates an awareness about the upcoming online projects in the above said fields. Nodhumi is a non-profitable platform which focuses on the upliftment of the society using Tamizh and technology
