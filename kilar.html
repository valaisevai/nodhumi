kilar | கிளர்

Tamizh language is one of the most ancient languages in the world having a very strong historical literature background. It enhances itself with enormous amount of creators and creations but in the case of research, it remains in vain. So "kilar" is being set up with the aim of reviving the ancient research in the language. In accordance with the present day context,"kilar" aims in reviving the ancient treasure of knowledge and developing many new researches to improve the scientific thinking in the language among the people. Beyond the fact that the researches of such as researchers, scholars, enthusiasts, experts are being carried out in this, a special focus is given here to expose the research ideas among the school students to improve this vision among them
